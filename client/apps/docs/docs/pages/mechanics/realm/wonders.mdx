# The Wonders

<img src="/buildings/wonder.png" alt="Wonder" width="200" className="float-left " />
### Wonders are exclusive structures within Eternum, with only 50 out of the 8,000 realms designated a Wonders.

## Exclusive Benefits

---

#### 🫏 **Cheaper Donkeys**

- Wonders produce donkeys at **10%** the Lords cost of a regular Realm at the same speed.

#### 🔮 **3x Starting Resources**

- Wonders start with **three** times the resources of a regular Realm, making them incredibly valuable at the very start
  of the Eternum journey.
