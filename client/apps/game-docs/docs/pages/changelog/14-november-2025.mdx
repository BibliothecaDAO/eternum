# 14th November 2025

## Documentation Updates

### Balance and Content Updates

- [Blitz/World Map & Movement](/blitz/worldmap-movement/movement): Updated the wheat consumption to **0.03/troop unit**
  for both exploration and travel.
- [Blitz/World Structures](/blitz/world-structures): Reduced Essence Rift discovery chance from **1/30** to **1/50** and
  increased essence output from **5 units/second** to **10 units/second**.
- [Blitz/Realms/Buildings](/blitz/realms/buildings): Population capacity from the Keep and Worker’s Huts has been
  increased from **+5** to **+6**. Construction costs for Farms, Worker’s Huts, T3 resource sites, and T2/T3 military
  buildings have been adjusted.
- [Blitz/Materials/Production](/blitz/materials/production): Wheat production from farms has been increased from **5
  units/second** to **6 units/second**. Wheat cost has been reduced from **4/second** to **3/second** for T2 troop
  production, and reduced from **7/second** to **4/second** for T3 troop production.
- [Blitz/Materials/Relics](/blitz/materials/relics): Removed Labor Production relics from the game due to incompatibility
  with the labor system in Blitz.
- [Blitz/Military/Stamina & Biomes](/blitz/military/stamina-and-biomes): Scorched biome combat bonuses have been changed
  to provide a damage bonus for Crossbowmen, a damage penalty for Paladins, and neither an advantage or penalty for
  Knights. Knights and Crossbowmen no longer have a stamina penalty on snow biomes.
- [Blitz/Realms/Realm](/blitz/realms/realm): Starting troop guidance now indicates that Realms on scorched biomes spawn
  Crossbowmen as their starting troop type. Essence costs for Realm upgrades have been revised.
- [Blitz/Victory](/blitz/victory): Victory Points awarded for exploring a tile have been reduced from **50 VP** to **25
  VP**.
