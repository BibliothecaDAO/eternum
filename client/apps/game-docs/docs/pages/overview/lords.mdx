# $LORDS Token

![LORDS Token](/images/resources/37.png)

$LORDS is the native token of the Realms Autonomous World. It serves as the primary currency within the Eternum
ecosystem, enabling economic activity and resource movement throughout the game.

## Token Details

- **Name**: LORDS
- **Network**: Starknet
- **Contract Address**:
  [0x0124aeb495b947201f5fac96fd1138e326ad86195b98df6dec9009158a533b49](https://starkscan.co/token/0x0124aeb495b947201f5fac96fd1138e326ad86195b98df6dec9009158a533b49)
- **Total Supply**: 300,000,000 LORDS

## $LORDS in Eternum

- **Trading Currency**: $LORDS serves as the primary currency for trading within Eternum's Automated Market Maker (AMM)
  and the peer-to-peer Orderbook, allowing Lords to facilitate trades and manage their economic interactions.

- **Transport System**: $LORDS are essential for producing donkeys, the backbone of Eternum's transport system,
  effectively acting as the "gas" token required for facilitating all in-game logistics and resource transfers.

## How to get $LORDS

### In Eternum

Within Eternum, Lords can obtain $LORDS through various methods:

- Selling surplus materials through the in-game AMM or Orderbook
- Defeating or negotiating with AI agents
- Discovering special quest tiles scattered throughout the Eternum world
- Achieving objectives in other Realms Ecosystem games

### On Starknet

Outside of gameplay, $LORDS can be purchased directly on the Starknet network via approved marketplaces or decentralized
exchanges.

You can use the following exchanges on Starknet to get $LORDS

- **[Ekubo](https://app.ekubo.org/?outputCurrency=LORDS&amount=1&inputCurrency=ETH)**: supports cartridge controller.
- **[Avnu](https://app.avnu.fi/en/eth-lords)**: doesn't support cartridge controller. You will need to get $LORDS using
  an [Argent](https://x.com/argentHQ) or [Braavos](https://x.com/myBraavos) wallet and then send them to your controller
  address.

Here's a [guide](https://x.com/lordcumberlord/status/1920650310621495350) to:

- Getting a Braavos wallet
- Bridging $ETH via Orbiter
- Swapping $ETH for $STRK & $LORDS
- Transferring $LORDS to your Cartridge Controller (and creating one if you don't have it yet)
