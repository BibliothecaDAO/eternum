Each storehouse adds {formatNumberWithCommas(ETERNUM_CONFIG().carryCapacityGram[CapacityConfig.Storehouse]/1000)}kg of storage capacity. Lords 
must be careful not to exceed the storage capacity of their Realms, as any produced materials that cannot be stored will be permanently lost.
